काव्य रीत
Event Organisers: Jatin Verma, Kavish Boyal, Vipasha Chandwani
Contacts: +91-8209208659, +91-8824135500


Zip your bag and unzip your creativity, for one of the most awaited annual poetry competition- काव्य रीत, is round the corner.

काव्य रीत
Participation fee: 50
Rules:
1. There will be only 1 round for the competition.
2. It is an individual event.
3. No stage props will be provided. In case the participant needs one, they may arrange it for themselves.
4. Language should be strictly Hindi or English, though the minimal use of Urdu/Persian is allowed.
5. Participants should stick to the allotted time limit of 4 minutes, exceeding it would result in NEGATIVE marking.
5. The judgement of the jury will be final, in any case. No recalibration requests will be entertained.

Whose line is it anyway?
(An Old Film Gets a New Script)
Event Organisers: Shivam Garg, Kavish Boyal
Contacts: +91-9582696699, +91-9079636335


It is a game in which two participants will watch a clip from a movie or a television show that has been muted. While the clip is shown on-screen, each participant will then write a dialogue for one of the characters following a given scene suggestion.

Whose line is it anyway?
Participation fee: 50
Rules:
1. The teams will be decided on the spot.
2. Each team member will be given a time 25 min to write their version of the story. No extra time will be allowed.
3. You can use either ENGLISH or HINDI as your medium of expression, but use only a single language.
4. Use of mobile phones, novels or any kind of written material will lead to the DISQUALIFICATION of the team.
5. Any kind of plagiarism will not go unnoticed.
6. The final decision lies with the organising team. In case of any dispute, its decision will be final.

Register for whose line is it anyway?

Caption It
(Caption Writing Competition)
Event Organisers: Jatin Verma, Sanatan Shrivastava
Contacts: +91-8824135500, +91-6261972313


Love captioning pictures over social media? Can cross limits for finding the correct caption? It’s time for you to show your creativity to write captions and show how many likes can you collect using your creative skills.

Caption It
Rules:
All the three photos are to be captioned Round 1 (Online round)--
1. Participant have to caption all of the photographs provided by the organisers.
2. Word limit of the caption- 50 words.
3. Each participant can only submit once.


Round 2 (Offline round)--
1. Only one picture will be shown to the finalists of round 1.
2. Word limit- 80 words.
3. Entries will be judged on humour, quality and overall impact of the caption.
4. Bonus points will be given to the participant who submits first.
Any kind of plagiarism in both the rounds is not allowed.
