Step-Up (Solo Dance)
Team Heads: Ashutosh Rajput, Shubham Sharma
Vice Presidents: Rahul Ashok Verma, Divyanshu Shrimali
Contacts:


STEP-UP (Solo Dance)
Participation fee: 50
Rules
1. Time limit is maximum 5 minutes. Negative points for exceeding the time limit.
2. The participants are advised to bring their song of choice in a pen drive.
3. The participants will be judged on their costumes, visual presentation and dance form.
4. Participants should get their own props. All props must be specified to the event coordinator.
5. Lighting of matchsticks, candles and cigarettes or any derogatory acts are strictly not allowed on stage.
6. Choice of song is open to the participants.
7. Pre-Register yourself on the website for participation.
8. Any obscene/offensive music/song is not allowed.

Drop the Mic (open mic event)
Team Heads: Ashutosh Rajput, Shubham Sharma
Vice Presidents: Rahul Ashok Verma, Divyanshu Shrimali
Contacts:


DROP THE MIC
Rules
1. It’s a solo competition and no registration fee requires.
2. Time allotted for each performance is maximum 4 minutes. Negative marking will be there in case of time exceeds the limit.
3. Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while performance.
4. The participants will be judged on the basis of content selection, voice quality, clarity, body language and their appeal to the audience.
5. The participants should bring their own musical instruments if required in the performance.
6. Choice of content is open to the participants but It should not have any slangs or derogatory language.
7. Pre-Register yourself on the website for participation.
